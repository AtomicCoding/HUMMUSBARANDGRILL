Our Story

Crafting authentic Mediterranean experiences since 2018

At Mezze Garden, we believe in the power of fresh, wholesome ingredients and time-honored traditions. Our signature hummus is prepared daily using authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish tells a story of Mediterranean culture, from our hand-selected tahini to our locally-sourced vegetables. We're committed to creating not just meals, but memorable experiences.
